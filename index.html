Arensal are looking to go one step further and claim a first premier league title in 21 years. The gunners have added to the leagues tighest defence with the addition of Italian international Ricardo Calafiori. Capable of operating at center back and left back, the Italian will provide brilliant cover and depth to challenge the starters.

Mikel Merino will play a similar role in the midfield, where he will provide competeition for Decland Rice, thomas Partey and Jorginho.The Spanish international can operate in a number of roles in Arteta's midfield and increases the overall quality and depth.

Deadline day signing Raheem Sterling will have a point to prove after a tough spell at Chelsea culminating in his abscence from the matchday squad. He will aim to get back into the England squad by impressing for Arsenal and will look to fight for his place in the team with Gabriel Martinelli and Leandro Trossard.

David Raya's move was made permanent after impressing on loan last season. He established himself as a starter and one of the best keppers in the division and will only build on his impressive perfromances. Neto has arrived on loan from Bournemouth to replace Aaron Ramsdale as the spaniard's backup.

Arsenal cashed in on academy graduates Emile Smith Rowe and Eddie Nketiah as the two seek more regular first team football. The fees will help Arsenal stay away from any finanical pressures including PSR regulations.

Aaron Ramsdale has departed for newly promoted Southampton after David Raya became the number one. Like Raheem Sterling the keeper will have a point to prove after a frustrating season personally. Fabio Vieira and Reiss Nelson have left on loan in serach of first team football with a chance to impress and try to break into the first team in the future.

After securing a long awaited return to champions league football, Aston Villa endured a very frantic start to the window as they raced to complete deals before the end of June, in order to comply with PSR rules. Villa fans knew one big name would have to leave in order to avoid a punishment and eventually a deal was agreed with Juventus for Douglas Luiz. Young Juventus players Enzo Barrenechea and Samuel Iling-Junior were a part of the deal but both have since moved out on loan in search of regular football.

The deals for Dobbin and Iroegbunam with Everton were also completed with PSR regulations in mind. Selling academy players is vital to maximise profits and stay within sustaainability guidlines. Villa later returned to Goodison Park and recruited Belgian international Amadou Onana who partners Youri Tielemans in a new look Belgian midfield.

Last summer's club record signing Moussa Diaby was surprisingly sold to Al-Ittihad in a move which was essential for Villa to comply for the champions league SCR (squad cost rules).

But Villa have strengthened the squad and added crucial depth required for a season in Europe's elite competition. Ian Maatsen arrived to add depth and youth at left back. Alex Moreno was subsequently loaned out.
